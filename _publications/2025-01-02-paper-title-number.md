This paper proposes novel methods for computing volumetric parameterizations of 3D simply-connected manifolds. Conventional surface parameterization approaches cannot control the bijectivity and local geometric distortions of the result mappings due to the complexity of solid manifolds. The proposed framework includes models for preserving geometric structure, achieving density equalization, and optimally balancing geometric and density distortions. The methods are validated through examples and manifold remeshing applications, demonstrating their effectiveness and accuracy.
